Rules

  1. The lettuce club meets once a semester, where everyone brings a head of lettuce and we race to see who can eat it the fastest.
  2. The winner will become the Head of Lettuce Club, and is responsible for arranging next year's meeting.
  3. BYOL (iceberg)
  4. No breaking up the lettuce prior to eating
  5. You do not need to eat the hard part
  6. Performance enhancing drugs allowed (“salad dressing”)
